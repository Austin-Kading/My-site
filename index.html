I belonged to a different group. A very important group that makes changes in your everyday life right in front of your eyes but works proudly behind the curtain. With great creativity and imagination and fullfilling our thoughts, needs and dreams through the www (World wide web). This is where I share my adventures with my cohorts and I to help shape the world we live in.

I have only been in the course for 2 weeks and now can do things and understand things that I really didn't think I would understand at this point. Throughout the course you will look at this page and see it grow and look more elegant and cunning but for 2 weeks, lets be honest, this is pretty good stuff. The cohorts are great and you learn so much from them! Let's point out what I feel has helped me and find pretty fascinating about the DBC experience.

  • Working through slack and communicating with my cohorts I haven't really had to go to a teacher for help because your cohorts are on top of it
  • The instructors are very helpful and supportive. Thus giving you mental strength and confidence to keep you going
  • The practice of empathy really is an amazing thing that DBC focuses on but is essential to the tech world. Collaborating on projects is what this business is all about. If you think you can lone ranger this you are wrong. Even brilliant ideas could always use a hand.
